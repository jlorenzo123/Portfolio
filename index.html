Hi, my name is John and I have been a System Administrator/ Middleware for CSC – Computer Science Corp. The accounts I worked on are General Dynamics, Chrysler and Boeing.

One of my responsibilities on the General Dynamics account was to maintain the WBCR Application – Workforce Business Control and Reporting. This consisted of an apache front end server that connected to a WebSphere portal server that connected to 3 back end servers – Content manager, Crystal Reports, DB2. I was responsible for daily operation of the system, incremental updates, backups and working with clients to understand basic use of system.

Some of my responsibilities on the Chrysler account was to maintain and troubleshoot Java Virtual Machines (JVMs), web instances, configure changes and updates for IBM HTTP Server (IHS), WAS WebSphere Application, Handling Problem Reports (PMRs) and Solving RCAs (Root Cause Analysis).

Some of my responsibilities on the Boeing account was to recycle and troubleshoot web instances, Installations of Apache, Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) and install of Web Single Sign On (WSSO)
